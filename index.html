Biodiversity refers to the incredible variety of life on Earth. An ecosystem is a dynamic community of living organisms (biotic) interacting with their physical environment (abiotic).

Loss of Genetic Diversity

Conservation Programs

Implementing conservation programs to protect endangered species and their habitats helps preserve genetic diversity.

Sustainable Practices

Promoting sustainable agricultural, fishing, and forestry practices reduces overexploitation and maintains ecosystem health.

Genetic Research

Investing in genetic research and breeding programs supports the maintenance of genetic diversity within species populations.

    

Over-Exploitation

Sustainable Harvesting

Implementing sustainable harvesting practices ensures that natural resources are used at a rate that allows ecosystems to regenerate.

Protected Areas

Establishing protected areas helps to conserve habitats and species by restricting human activities that lead to overexploitation.

Community Engagement

Involving local communities in conservation efforts promotes sustainable resource management and biodiversity protection.
